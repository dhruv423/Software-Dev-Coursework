Calculate the arrival date starting from the current position and traveling towards position p at s kilometers per day. 

First calculate the distance between current position and desired position (p), then divide the distance by s to get days it takes to reach that position. Finally use the DateT method add\+\_\+days to add the days needed to travel the distance. The reason I took the ceil of days is because I want to give the date that they will reach the position by.
